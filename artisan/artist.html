Visual Artist - Jose 'Jao Jr.' Orola

Born in Vancouver, British Columbia, Jose Orola has been practicing art since the age of 7. First being introduced to the practice of paper machet by his renowned uncle, Senior Jao, his love for art stems from his familial connections. This introduction sparked a lifelong passion, blending traditional techniques with his own evolving style.

Over the years, Jose has explored various mediums, including painting, sculpture, and mixed media, drawing inspiration from his multicultural upbringing. His work often reflects themes of identity, memory, and connection, paying homage to his family's legacy while forging his unique artistic path.

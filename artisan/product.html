Piñata Redesign Overview

A personal passion project inspired by *Piñata*, the iconic album by Freddie Gibbs and Madlib. This redesign reimagines the album's visual identity across multiple touchpoints, including packaging, cover art, and merchandise. Through bold illustrations, textured design elements, and vibrant color palettes, the project pays homage to the album's raw, unfiltered energy while introducing a fresh, contemporary aesthetic. The result is a cohesive and versatile visual system that enhances the album's storytelling and expands its presence beyond music into tangible, collectible art.

This project included creating new album packaging, cover art, and accompanying merchandise. The redesign process focused on blending bold, vibrant colors with textured, hand-drawn illustrations that reflect the album's dynamic soundscape. I wanted the visuals to evoke the same sense of nostalgia and grit that the music does, while also offering a fresh take that appeals to both longtime fans and new listeners. In addition to the cover, I explored the possibilities of extending the album's branding to merchandise like vinyl sleeves, posters, and apparel. This exercise was not only a tribute to the artists but also a way to challenge my skills in creating cohesive, multi-format design systems.

Virtual Angel

One of my earliest sculpting projects, this clay headless angel embodies both imperfection and artistic growth. With delicate wings and a flowing robe, the figure stands frozen in an unfinished, almost haunting beauty—a reflection of my learning process at the time. Though headless, it carries an eerie elegance, a reminder that creativity often thrives in the unexpected.
